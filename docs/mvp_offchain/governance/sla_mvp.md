# SLA MVP

## Severity levels
- Critical: service unavailable, ledger inconsistency, severe data loss risk.
- High: core flow degraded (quiz/attempt/redeem failing for significant users).
- Medium: partial degradation, workaround available.
- Low: cosmetic/non-blocking issue.

## Response and resolution targets
| Severity | First response | Target resolution |
|---|---|---|
| Critical | 1h | <24h |
| High | 4h | <48h |
| Medium | 1 business day | <5 business days |
| Low | 2 business days | next sprint |

## Escalation
- Critical: immediate founder + freelance sync and traffic guardrails.
- High: assign owner in same day and publish mitigation.

## SLA exclusions
- Third-party feed outage outside control (covered by stale mode policy).
